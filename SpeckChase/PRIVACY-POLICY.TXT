Speck Chase Privacy Policy

It's quite simple: The Speck Chase application does not collect any personal or sensitive data.
Specifically:
* No data regarding the person who runs the application is collected.
* No data regarding the device on which the application runs is collected.
* No data regarding the circumstances in which the application is run is collected.
* Actually, no data is handled by the Speck Chase application at all, besides the clicks made by the user on the screen made during the gameplay.

As nothing is collected, there is nothing to keep, protect, delete, or worry of leaking.
That's all folks.

Enjoy your play!
